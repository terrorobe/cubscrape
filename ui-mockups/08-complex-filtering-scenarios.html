Common Filter Logic Patterns

AND Logic (Restrictive)

Use Case: Specific Discovery
"I want puzzle games that are also indie AND have been covered by Retro Gaming channel"
Filter Chain:
Tags: Puzzle
AND
Tags: Indie
AND
Channel: Retro Gaming
Result: 23 highly specific games

OR Logic (Expansive)

Use Case: Broad Discovery
"Show me games that are either puzzle OR strategy, from any popular channel"
Filter Chain:
Tags: Puzzle
OR
Tags: Strategy
AND
Channel: Popular (>10K subs)
Result: 1,247 broader selection
